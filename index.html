Random Spotify album selector

Add random saved album(s) to your queue.

More information about this application.

No personnal information is retrieve by the applciation and all your data stay safe in your browser.
This application require the minimal permission possible on your spotify account. It only use the user-library-read and the user-modify-playback-state permissions that allows to ready your spotify library to pick the albums or favorite tracks and add them to your playlist.


This application was created with ❤️ by maggick and is open source.


This application was inspired by Paul Almeida-Seele Spotify Album Selector.

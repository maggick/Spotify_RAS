Random Spotify album selector

Add random saved album(s) directly to your playing queue in a breeze.

This application is still in development mode and I need to add each user to the application manually. If you want to use it contact me with your Spotify username and email address.
More information about this application.

This application was inspired by Paul Almeida-Seele Spotify Album Selector and created with ❤️ and ☕ by maggick.


No personal information is retrieved by the application and all your data stay safe in your browser. This application require the minimal permission possible on your Spotify account and only use the user-library-read and the user-modify-playback-state permissions that allow to read your Spotify library to pick the albums or favorite tracks and add them to your playlist.


If you want to revoke access for this tool, go to your Approved Applications in your Spotify profile. Look for Random Album Selector and click on the Revoke Access button beside it.
